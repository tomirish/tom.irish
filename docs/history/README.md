# Design History

A visual record of every major redesign of Tom Irish's personal website since 2013.

| Version | Year | Platform | Domain |
|---|---|---|---|
| [v1](#v1--2013) | 2013 | Weebly | tomirish.me |
| [v2](#v2--2015) | 2015 | Weebly | tomirish.me |
| [v3](#v3--early-2018) | Early 2018 | Weebly | tomirish.me |
| [v4](#v4--late-2018) | Late 2018 | HTML5 UP template | tom.irish |
| [v5](#v5--2019) | 2019 | Carrd.co | tom.irish |
| [v6](#v6--2024) | 2024 | Carrd.co | tom.irish |
| [v7](#v7--july-2025) | July 2025 | Carrd.co | tom.irish |
| [v8](#v8--2025) | 2025 | Custom (GitHub + Cloudflare Pages) | tom.irish |

---

## v1 — 2013

![v1](v1-2013.png)

Personal/family site built on Weebly. Cursive "Tom Irish" logo, full-width family photo banner, dark navy navigation bar. Content covered family life: Home, Photos, Blog, Geocaching, About Me. The first iteration of tomirish.me.

---

## v2 — 2015

![v2](v2-2015.png)

Still on Weebly but pivoted to a professional focus. Shamrock logo, bold green "TOM IRISH" wordmark, white background, circular headshot, social icon row. Navigation reduced to Home and Resume.

---

## v3 — Early 2018

![v3](v3-2018a.png)

Refinement of v2 on the same Weebly platform. Shamrock logo dropped, green accent reduced to thin rule lines top and bottom, serif headline type, single-column layout. Cleaner and more restrained than v2.

---

## v4 — Late 2018

![v4](v4-2018b.png)

Moved to the tom.irish domain. Built on an HTML5 UP template. Dark full-screen panels, Source Sans Pro typography, sectioned layout: Home, Resume, About.

---

## v5 — 2019

![v5](v5-2019.png)

Switched to Carrd.co. White background, wide-spaced caps typography, steel blue accent, circular headshot centered on the page. Navigation as pill-shaped outline buttons. Minimal and modern.

---

## v6 — 2024

![v6](v6-2024.png)

Redesigned within Carrd.co. Steel blue card with rounded corners, same headshot and caps type, hexagon social icon row without icon glyphs inside. Moodier and more graphic than v5.

---

## v7 — July 2025

![v7](v7-2025a.png)

Second Carrd.co card design. Dark charcoal background, new headshot (suit, red tie), hexagon icons now filled with glyphs (resume, email, phone, LinkedIn). Same card format, darker palette.

---

## v8 — 2025

![v8](v8-2025b.png)

Complete departure from the card format. First fully custom-built version — Markdown-driven pipeline, deployed via GitHub Actions to Cloudflare Pages. Editorial serif name (Playfair Display), rounded photo left-aligned, crimson accent rule and Resume button, off-white background. New headshot. Resume PDF generated at build time.
